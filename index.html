Clout Collector!!

You will be given a random number goal at the start of the game.

There are four chains below. By clicking on a chain, you will add a specific amount of points to your total clout.

You win the game by matching your total clout to the random number goal, you lose the game if your total clout goes above the random number goal.

The value of each chain is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, it will change the values of each chain.
